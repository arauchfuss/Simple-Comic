Quicklook Plugin

To allow comics to better integrate with Finder Simple Comic has a bundled Quicklook plugin. Any files with a cbz or cbr extension will have automatically generated thumbnails and previews.

What are cbr and cbz Files?

CBR and CBZ stand for Comic Book Rar and Comic Book Zip respectively. They are simply rar and zip files that have been renamed to differentiate them from non-comic archives.

Why Only cbr or cbz?

Simple Comic would create thumbnails and previews for all zip and rar archives, even those not comic related.

Choosing a thumbnail image

Simple Comic assumes that the first page of an archive is the cover and sets the thumbnail accordingly. If this is incorrect you can use the Set Page as Icon command.
